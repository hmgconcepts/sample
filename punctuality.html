⏱ Punctuality Points

Encourage punctuality: students who check in at/before the deadline and check out during the closing period earn points every school day. Points build up across the term and — only if the school wants — can be pushed into the students' Results (any report-card column) exactly like CBT scores. Check-ins/outs come from the student QR check-in page (student_clock).

⚙️ Scoring rules

0 = strict (both required)
Times are compared in your server's timezone. Saved in punctuality_config.

⚡ Compute daily awards

Run after morning check-in, and again after closing — re-running the same day simply re-grades it (late check-outs still earn their point afterwards). Tip: compute each school day once the gate closes.
